How to make Apple Fritters



Ingredients

For the fritters
Vegetable oil
1½ cups all-purpose flour
3 tablespoons granulated sugar
2½ teaspoons baking powder
1 teaspoon salt
½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
¾ cup whole milk
2 large eggs
2 teaspoons vanilla extract
2 large apples
For the glaze
2 cups powdered sugar
3-4 tablespoons whole milk
½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

For the fritters
Heat the oil in a large pot or Dutch oven, 1½ inches deep, to 350°F
Line a large baking sheet with paper towels and place a wire rack over it
Whisk together in a bowl: flour, sugar, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon
Whisk in a different bowl: milk, eggs, and vanilla. Pour this into the dry mixture and mix.
Add the apples and coat them evenly
Once the oil is 350°F, scoop ¼ cup of the batter into the hot oil and fry until golden brown on both sides
Transfer to the wire rack to cool
For the glaze
Whisk together: powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla
Dip the fritters or drizzle using a spoon
