How to make Caramel Apples



Ingredients

8-9 cold apples
1 and 3/4 cups heavy cream
1 cup light corn syrup
2 cups brown sugar
1/4 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

Instructions

Rinse the apples and wipe dry. Remember to remove the stem and insert a stick 3/4 deep
Line a large baking sheet with a silicone mat or grease the pan. Do not use paper as the caramel will stick to it.
Combine the heavy cream, corn syrup, brown sugar, butter, and salt in a saucepan over medium heat
Once the caramel reaches 235°F – 240°F, remove it from the heat and quickly stir in the vanilla. Avoid over-stirring. Allow to cool until it thickens.
Tilt the pot to coat all sides when you dip the apple. Let the excess caramel to drip off then place the apples on the baking sheet. Add toppings if desires before setting it aside to set.
